# Global Business Rules — Meridian Consulting Group

These rules apply across ALL SaaS categories. Category-specific rules supplement these;
they do not override them.

## Mandatory Compliance
- SOC2 Type II certification: REQUIRED. No exceptions for client-data tools.
- UK GDPR / EU GDPR: Data residency must be UK or EU. US-only storage is a hard block.
- SSO (SAML 2.0 or OIDC): Required for any tool with more than 10 user seats.
- MFA enforcement: Must be enforceable at admin level.
- Audit logging: Required for finance, HR, CRM. Logs must be exportable.

## Financial Thresholds
- Minimum net annual saving to justify switch: £1,200/yr
- Maximum acceptable migration cost: £5,000 (staff time + setup)
- Amortise migration cost over 3 years when calculating ROI
- 3-year vendor lock-in contracts require board sign-off — flag in verdict

## Migration Constraints
- Maximum staff time budget per migration: 15 hours
- Blended staff rate: £48/hr (use unless role specified)
- Parallel running period required: minimum 2 weeks before full cutover
- Integrations must be validated before cutover — no broken-integration launches

## Vendor Stability Signals (treat as push factors)
- Acquisition notice: flag as HOLD pending roadmap clarity
- Pricing model change (per-seat to usage-based): recalculate at current usage
- EOL or deprecation notice on a used feature: treat as HIGH push signal
- Two or more missed roadmap commitments: treat as MEDIUM push signal

## Preferred Contract Terms
- Monthly or annual preferred
- Multi-year only with board approval
- Exit clause or data export guarantee required
