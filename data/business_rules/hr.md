# Business Rules — HR Category

## Must-Have Features
- Leave and absence management
- Employee self-service portal
- Document storage with version control (contracts, policies)
- UK payroll integration (PayAxis native preferred — middleware broke twice)
- GDPR right-to-erasure automation (manual process is compliance risk)
- Onboarding workflow templates
- Org chart

## Nice-to-Have Features
- Continuous performance feedback (replace annual review cycle)
- Pulse surveys / eNPS
- Learning management system (LMS) with course builder
- Compensation benchmarking
- AI-assisted job description generator

## Needs / Pain Points (active)
- GDPR erasure is manual — legal flagged this as compliance risk Q3 2024
- Onboarding setup takes 2.5 hrs per new hire due to no templates
- Performance management not adopted — team using Google Forms instead
- PayAxis middleware connector breaks after vendor updates (~quarterly)

## Compliance
- Employee data — GDPR mandatory, UK data residency preferred
- SOC2 Type II required
- Audit log for HR data access and changes

## Pricing Sensitivity
- Current: £385/mo (150 seats, £2.57/seat)
- Per-seat pricing at this scale: anything above £4/seat needs strong justification
- Flat-rate pricing for unlimited seats would be attractive

## Migration Notes
- Full employee record history must migrate (employment history, documents, leave records)
- Integration with PayAxis must be native — no middleware
- Parallel running during migration: minimum 4 weeks given payroll risk
