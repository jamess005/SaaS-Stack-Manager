# Business Rules — Finance Category

## Must-Have Features
- Multi-currency invoicing and payments (EUR is active requirement)
- Bank reconciliation with real-time Barclays Business feed
- VAT / Making Tax Digital (MTD) compliance
- UK payroll sync (PayAxis)
- IFRS 15 compliant revenue recognition
- Audit trail export: PDF with digital signature (auditor requirement)
- 24/7 support SLA

## Nice-to-Have Features
- AI anomaly detection on transactions
- Real-time P&L dashboards
- Project-based billing and profitability tracking
- Multi-entity / group consolidation
- Cash flow forecasting with scenario modelling

## Needs / Pain Points (active)
- Multi-currency gap is blocking EU client invoicing — highest priority
- Month-end reconciliation bottleneck (8–12 mins on large datasets)
- IFRS 15 workaround in use — audit risk
- Barclays bank feed is overnight only — no intraday visibility

## Compliance
- SOC2 Type II mandatory
- Financial data: UK/EU residency only
- Full audit trail with export capability
- GDPR for any client financial data

## Pricing Sensitivity
- Current: £420/mo (8 seats)
- Acceptable range: £350–£600/mo
- High willingness to pay for multi-currency + real-time banking given business impact

## Migration Notes
- 3 years of financial history must migrate
- Parallel running: minimum 1 full month-end cycle before cutover
- PayAxis integration must be validated in staging before go-live
