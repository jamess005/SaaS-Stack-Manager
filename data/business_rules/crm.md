# Business Rules — CRM Category

## Must-Have Features
- Contact and company management with activity timeline
- Deal pipeline with customisable stages
- Email integration (send/receive/log from Gmail and Outlook)
- Native or first-party LinkedIn enrichment (manual copy is a known pain point)
- Reporting: deal velocity, pipeline value, win/loss by rep
- API for bidirectional sync with InsightDeck (analytics)
- Mobile app: iOS and Android, stable

## Nice-to-Have Features
- AI-assisted lead scoring
- Email sequence automation (unlimited steps preferred)
- Meeting scheduler with calendar sync
- Conversation intelligence (call recording + transcript)
- Client health score dashboard

## Needs / Pain Points (active)
- LinkedIn enrichment gap is causing 3–4 hrs/week of manual data entry across sales team
- Email sequence cap at 5 steps limits nurture campaigns
- API rate limits breaking InsightDeck sync — priority fix needed

## Compliance
- Client data stored in CRM — SOC2 Type II mandatory
- GDPR: EU client contact data must remain in EU/UK
- Audit log: required for client data access events

## Pricing Sensitivity
- Current: £510/mo (22 seats)
- Acceptable range: £400–£650/mo for equivalent seat count
- Per-seat pricing preferred over flat-rate for this team size

## Migration Notes
- Pipeline stages and custom fields must be fully portable (CSV export minimum)
- Email history: 12 months minimum must migrate
- Integration with VaultLedger and InsightDeck must be validated pre-cutover
